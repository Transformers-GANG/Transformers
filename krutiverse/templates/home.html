About Our Project

Developed by Team Transformers, this Smart Blood Donation app revolutionizes blood donation management with:

  • AI-powered real-time donor-recipient matching (considering blood group, health history, allergies, insulin)
  • Emergency blood request alerts system
  • OpenCV verification for secure authentication
  • Twilio-powered messaging and alerts
  • Location-based matching for efficient donations
  • User profiles with donation history tracking
